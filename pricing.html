FAQ

Do you provide the filters?

You can supply your own, or we can bring the correct replacements depending on your plan/setup.

Is there a contract

We ask for an initial 6-month commitment to cover the cost of your first filter. After that, your plan is month-to-month and you can cancel anytime.

Why 6 months?

Certain home filters last up to 6 months and are significantly more expensive. This allows us to provide the filter upfront while keeping your monthly cost low.

What areas do you serve?

Currently focused on Utah County. If you’re nearby, still reach out and we’ll let you know.

What if I’m not sure what filters I need?

No problem. Choose “Custom” or “Not sure” when signing up and we’ll help identify the right items.
